Hi, it's Jingxuan Cui 崔静璇


I am a Ph.D. candidate at Nanjing University of Information Science & Technology (NUIST), working on the tropical climate dynamics, tropical-extratropical interaction and climate change.

Research 研究相关

I am focus on the changes in the Madden-Jullian Oscillation (MJO), its teleconnections and its global climate influences under global warming, working with Dr. Tim Li and Dr. Lu Wang. Now I am visiting at Colorado State University and working with Dr. Eric Maloney doing researches about the projection of the MJO teleconnection in a warmer climate.
